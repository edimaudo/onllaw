onllaw

Democratizing access to employment contract reviews in Ontario

Empowering residents of Ontario, Canada with the ability to identify employment contracts issues before they are signed.

The Problem

•Ontario employees often sign contracts containing non-compliant "enforceability" clauses.
•Most Legal reviews are unaffordable for individual employees (100+ CAD per hour).
•Manual review of employment contracts are time-prohibitive.

How it's Built

Backend

FastAPI
Airia Platform
Vercel

Frontend

Jinja2 Templates
Tailwind CSS




Key Features & Demo

•Compliance Reviews: Generates legal summary, enforceability status, applicable legal sections, next steps, email drafts.
•Employment Standard Q&A chatbot: Curious about Employment Standards? You can now ask questions via a chatbot.
•Lawyer References: Has a list of Ontario lawyers focused on employment and labour law.

Next Steps

•Lawyers: Expand list of lawyers to all of Canada.
